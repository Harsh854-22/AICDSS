Yoga Poses
Downward Dog

Benefits: Strengthens the entire body, stretches the hamstrings and calves

Instructions: Start on hands and knees, lift hips, and straighten legs while pressing heels towards the floor

Tips: Keep your hands shoulder-width apart and feet hip-width apart

Warrior II

Benefits: Strengthens legs, opens hips and chest

Instructions: Stand with legs wide, turn one foot out, bend knee, extend arms to sides

Tips: Ensure knee is aligned with ankle, engage core

Tree Pose

Benefits: Improves balance and stability, strengthens legs

Instructions: Stand on one leg, place other foot on inner thigh, bring hands together at chest

Tips: Focus on a fixed point to maintain balance

Benefits: Stretches the back, hips, and thighs, relaxes the body

Instructions: Kneel on the floor, sit back on heels, extend arms forward and lower forehead to the ground

Tips: Breathe deeply and relax into the pose

Bridge Pose

Benefits: Strengthens the glutes and lower back, opens the chest

Instructions: Lie on your back, bend knees, lift hips, and press feet into the floor

Tips: Keep your shoulders on the ground and clasp hands under your back

Cat-Cow Pose

Benefits: Increases flexibility of the spine, warms up the body

Instructions: Start on hands and knees, alternate between arching your back and dipping your belly

Tips: Move slowly and synchronize breath with movement

Cobra Pose

Benefits: Stretches the chest and abdomen, strengthens the spine

Instructions: Lie face down, place hands under shoulders, lift chest while keeping pelvis on the ground

Tips: Keep elbows close to your body and gaze slightly upward

Seated Forward Bend

Benefits: Stretches the hamstrings and lower back

Instructions: Sit with legs extended, reach forward towards toes while keeping back straight

Tips: Bend at the hips rather than the waist to avoid straining the back

Plank Pose

Benefits: Strengthens core, arms, and legs

Instructions: Start in a push-up position, keep body in a straight line from head to heels

Tips: Engage your core and keep your body aligned

Legs Up the Wall

Benefits: Relieves tired legs, improves circulation

Instructions: Lie on your back with legs extended up the wall, relax your arms by your sides

Tips: Use a folded blanket under your hips for added support

Vajrasana

Benefits: Improves digestion, helps with relaxation

Instructions: Kneel on the floor, sit back on heels, keeping your back straight

Tips: Ensure your feet are close together and your knees are apart

Dhanurasana

Benefits: Stretches the front body, strengthens the back

Instructions: Lie on your stomach, bend knees, hold ankles, and lift chest and thighs off the ground

Tips: Keep breathing evenly while in the pose

Mritasana

Benefits: Enhances flexibility and core strength

Instructions: Lie on your back, bend knees, and hold your feet while pressing knees towards the floor

Tips: Maintain a gentle stretch without forcing the knees down

Ustrasana

Benefits: Opens the chest, stretches the front of the body

Surya Namaskar

Benefits: A full-body workout, improves flexibility, strength, and balance

Instructions: A series of poses performed in a sequence, starting with a standing pose, moving into forward bends, and finishing with backbends

Tips: Coordinate breathing with movements and perform the sequence slowly
