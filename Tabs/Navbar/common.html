Most Common Diseases
Malaria

Medicine: Antimalarial drugs (e.g., Chloroquine, Artemisinin)

Diet: High-calorie diet with plenty of fluids and nutrients

Exercise: Light exercises like walking and stretching

Yoga: Pranayama (breathing exercises), relaxation techniques

Diabetes

Medicine: Insulin, Metformin, Sulfonylureas

Diet: Low-carb, high-fiber diet; monitor sugar intake

Exercise: Regular aerobic exercises (e.g., walking, cycling)

Yoga: Surya Namaskar, Bhujangasana, Dhanurasana

Kidney Diseases

Medicine: ACE inhibitors, diuretics, antibiotics

Diet: Low-sodium, low-protein diet; adequate hydration

Exercise: Light to moderate exercises (e.g., walking, swimming)

Yoga: Paschimottanasana, Pawanmuktasana, Shalabhasana

Cancer

Medicine: Chemotherapy, targeted therapy, immunotherapy

Diet: Balanced diet with fruits, vegetables, and lean protein

Exercise: Low-impact exercises (e.g., walking, yoga)

Yoga: Meditation, deep relaxation, restorative yoga

Tuberculosis

Medicine: Antibiotics (e.g., Isoniazid, Rifampicin)

Diet: Protein-rich diet with vitamins and minerals

Exercise: Gentle exercises (e.g., walking, breathing exercises)

Yoga: Pranayama, Sukhasana, Matsyasana

Dengue

Medicine: Pain relievers (e.g., Acetaminophen); avoid NSAIDs

Diet: High-fluid intake; fruits like papaya and kiwi

Exercise: Rest and recovery; light stretching

Yoga: Deep breathing exercises, relaxation techniques

Hypertension

Medicine: Diuretics, beta-blockers, ACE inhibitors

Diet: Low-sodium diet; increase fruits and vegetables

Exercise: Regular physical activities (e.g., walking, swimming)

Yoga: Shavasana, Balasana, Setu Bandhasana

Chikungunya

Medicine: Pain relievers; avoid aspirin and NSAIDs

Diet: Hydration with fluids; nutrient-rich diet

Exercise: Gentle stretching; focus on recovery

Yoga: Pranayama, gentle yoga poses for joint relief

Asthma

Medicine: Inhalers, bronchodilators, corticosteroids

Diet: Anti-inflammatory foods; avoid allergens

Exercise: Moderate activities; swimming is beneficial

Yoga: Bhramari Pranayama, Anulom Vilom, Ardha Matsyendrasana

Typhoid

Medicine: Antibiotics (e.g., Ciprofloxacin, Azithromycin)

Diet: High-calorie, bland diet; adequate hydration

Exercise: Rest during the acute phase; light activity post-recovery

Yoga: Pranayama, meditation for relaxation

Hepatitis

Medicine: Antiviral medications, liver support therapy

Diet: Low-fat, high-fiber diet; avoid alcohol

Exercise: Light physical activities; focus on energy conservation

Yoga: Gentle yoga, deep breathing exercises

Heart Disease

Medicine: Statins, beta-blockers, ACE inhibitors

Diet: Heart-healthy diet; low in saturated fats and cholesterol

Exercise: Regular cardio exercises (e.g., brisk walking, cycling)

Yoga: Tadasana, Vrikshasana, Adho Mukha Svanasana
