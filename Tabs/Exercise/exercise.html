Exercise Guide
Plank Hold

Benefits: Strengthens lower body

Benefits: Strengthens core, improves posture

Instructions: Hold a plank position with forearms on the ground and body in a straight line.

Tips: Keep your hips level and avoid dropping them.

Jumping Jack

Benefits: Increases cardiovascular fitness, warms up the body

Instructions: Jump with legs spreading out and arms raising overhead, then return to the starting position.

Tips: Keep a steady rhythm and land softly.

Crunches

Benefits: Targets abdominal muscles

Instructions: Lie on your back with knees bent and lift your upper body towards your knees.

Tips: Avoid pulling on your neck; use your abs to lift.

Leg Raises

Benefits: Strengthens lower abs

Instructions: Lie flat on your back and lift your legs to a 90-degree angle, then lower them slowly.

Tips: Keep your lower back pressed into the ground.

Russian Twist

Benefits: Works the oblique muscles

Instructions: Sit with knees bent, lean back slightly, and twist your torso to each side.

Tips: Keep your back straight and engage your core.

Jumping Jack

Instructions: Jump with legs spreading out and arms raising overhead, then return to the starting position.

Tips: Keep a steady rhythm and land softly.

Incline Push-ups

Benefits: Targets upper chest and triceps

Instructions: Perform push-ups with your hands elevated on a bench or platform.

Tips: Keep your body in a straight line and avoid arching your back.

Knee Push-ups

Benefits: Strengthens chest and triceps with reduced intensity

Instructions: Perform push-ups with knees on the ground.

Tips: Maintain a straight line from your knees to your head.

Push-ups

Benefits: Builds upper body strength

Instructions: Perform push-ups with your hands shoulder-width apart and body straight.

Tips: Keep your elbows at a 45-degree angle from your body.

Wide Arm Push-ups

Benefits: Focuses on the chest and shoulders

Instructions: Perform push-ups with hands wider than shoulder-width.

Tips: Engage your core and keep your back straight.

Squats

Instructions: Stand with feet shoulder-width apart and lower your body by bending your knees and hips, then return to standing.

Tips: Keep your knees aligned with your toes and chest up.

Lunge

Benefits: Targets quads, hamstrings, and glutes

Instructions: Step forward with one leg and lower your body until both knees are bent at 90 degrees.

Tips: Keep your front knee aligned with your ankle and avoid letting it extend past your toes.

Sumo Squat

Benefits: Emphasizes inner thighs and glutes

Instructions: Perform a squat with your feet wider than shoulder-width and toes pointed outward.

Tips: Push through your heels and keep your chest upright.

Donkey Kick

Benefits: Strengthens glutes and core

Instructions: On all fours, lift one leg towards the ceiling while keeping your knee bent.

Tips: Avoid arching your back and keep your core engaged.

Jumping Jack

Benefits: Increases cardiovascular fitness, warms up the body

Instructions: Jump with legs spreading out and arms raising overhead, then return to the starting position.

Tips: Keep a steady rhythm and land softly.

Triceps Dips

Benefits: Targets triceps and shoulders

Instructions: Use a bench or chair to support your hands and lower your body by bending your elbows, then push back up.

Tips: Keep your elbows close to your body and avoid letting your shoulders hunch.

Diamond Push-ups

Benefits: Focuses on triceps and inner chest

Instructions: Perform push-ups with hands close together, forming a diamond shape with your fingers.

Tips: Keep your body in a straight line and avoid flaring your elbows out.

Punches

Benefits: Enhances arm endurance and coordination

Instructions: Throw punches in the air while maintaining a steady rhythm.

Tips: Engage your core and keep your movements controlled.

Push-ups

Benefits: Builds upper body strength

Instructions: Perform push-ups with your hands shoulder-width apart and body straight.

Tips: Keep your elbows at a 45-degree angle from your body.
